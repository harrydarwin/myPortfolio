About Me

Team Player

A unique background creates a unique perspective

I am a highly-versatile operations manager turned developer with a wealth of experience in concept development and project management in the Retail and Food&Beverage industry. I bring experience developing responsive web applications using Javascript, React, HTML, CSS

Whether through physical or digital space, my goal is always to turn good ideas into beautiful user experiences that people want to share.

Previously my work has been both client facing and largely data-based. I wanted to bring my skills into a field where I could apply them as well as bring some larger scale creativity and useability to my work. I believe web development provides an opportunity to connect with more people in a more meaningful way

Since 2010 I have assisted in opening over 10 restaurants and 1 retail operation. Some of these have been close to home or in the United States and, others as far away as Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. As a learning addict, I transitioned to work with a Toronto e-commerce startup in 2019 and had an excellent run opening their first large scale, long term, pop-up, retail location (which has since been torn down for condo construction). I left that role in 2020 after the pop-up was finished, and took some time to consider my next move. I kept busy by completing a course in SQL, courses in basic HMTL, CSS and Javascript as well as completing a certificates for scrum agile methodologies (scrum master certification) and a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt certification (process improvement).

Whatever I decide to do, I do with drive and I take it upon myself to learn everything I can along the way.
